A growing number of companies are pretraining their own models. Data control and GPU economics are pushing companies on-prem. But going on-prem means taking on everything a cloud provider does invisibly with none of their tooling.

Sixtytwo is the observability infrastructure layer for on-prem. Cloud providers absorb hardware failure and capacity volatility. On‑prem, we build the automation for health detection, quarantine, rollouts, spares, and capacity planning to get to cloud-level reliability.

Sixtytwo connects data at all layers for on-prem clusters. On-prem clusters get the same system-wide visibility that cloud providers surface automatically.

The future of AI runs on-prem.
