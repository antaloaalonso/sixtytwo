The AI infrastructure gap is widening.

Every enterprise wants to run AI on their own hardware. For privacy. For control. For compliance. But going from cloud to on-prem is brutally hard, and the tooling to get there doesn't exist.

Companies are spending millions on GPU clusters they can barely set up, let alone monitor or optimize. The path from "we bought the hardware" to "we're running models in production" is undefined. No migration playbooks. No deployment infrastructure. No observability. Just expensive hardware and hope.

We guide companies through the entire journey—from cloud-dependent to fully operational on-prem AI. We handle the deployment, build the observability layer, manage fleet health, orchestrate workloads, and run the container lifecycle. End to end.

Cloud providers spent a decade building world-class infrastructure tooling for themselves. We're building the equivalent for everyone else.

This is not a nice-to-have. As open-source models mature and data sovereignty regulations tighten, on-prem deployment becomes inevitable. The companies that move first will own this layer of the stack.

We are a small team of engineers who have operated GPU infrastructure at scale. We know what breaks during migration, what's missing from the on-prem stack, and what needs to exist.

The future of AI runs on-prem. We're building the infrastructure to make it work.
