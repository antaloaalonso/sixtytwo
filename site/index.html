The AI infrastructure gap is widening.

Every enterprise wants to run AI on their own hardware. For privacy. For control. For compliance. But the tooling doesn't exist.

Cloud providers have spent a decade building world-class observability, orchestration, and fleet management for their own infrastructure. None of that exists for on-prem AI deployments.

Companies are spending millions on GPU clusters they can barely monitor, let alone optimize. They're flying blind—no telemetry, no health management, no intelligent scheduling. Just expensive hardware and hope.

We're building the control plane for on-prem AI infrastructure. Deep observability. Fleet health management. Intelligent orchestration. Container lifecycle management. Everything enterprises need to run AI on their own terms.

This is not a nice-to-have. As open-source models mature and data sovereignty regulations tighten, on-prem deployment becomes inevitable. The companies that move first will own this layer of the stack.

We are a small team of engineers who have operated GPU infrastructure at scale. We know what breaks, what's missing, and what needs to exist.

The future of AI runs on-prem. We're building the infrastructure to make it work.
